What do you have planned for after graduation?
I want to pursue design that includes all peoples and cultures. My dream job would be creating graphics for display and apparel for sports or creating user interface designs.

What was your favorite class you took at SDSU?
My favorite class I took at SDSU would be my experiential design class. I created a strong brand with a unique message, and created a siteplan for a 3d immersive experience .

I am proud of the graphics I create since I put so much effort and detail into them. The most proud project I've worked on would have to be this website because I learned so many new things and challenged myself a designer

How was your Exchange Program in Seoul, South Korea?
